## Flagged Issues

### Errors

- [ERROR] "語言模型 10 項評測指標：安全性、可解釋性、**韌性**、公平性、準確性、透明性、**問責性**、可靠性、隱私、資安"
  - **What's wrong**: Two indicator names use incorrect Chinese terms. Official CNA and 數位發展部 sources consistently use **「彈性」** (not 韌性) for Resiliency, and **「當責性」** (not 問責性) for Accountability.
  - **Fix**: "安全性、可解釋性、**彈性**、公平性、準確性、透明性、**當責性**、可靠性、隱私、資安"
  - **Source**: [中央社 2023/12/06](https://www.cna.com.tw/news/ait/202312060129.aspx), [TechNews](https://technews.tw/2023/12/07/ai-product-and-system-evaluation-center-launch/)
  - **Exam risk**: HIGH -- students memorizing "韌性" and "問責性" would select the wrong answer on a multiple-choice question about the 10 indicators

- [ERROR] "風險管理：...數位發展部負責建立與國際接軌之**風險管理框架**"
  - **What's wrong**: The law (第16條) says **「風險分類框架」** (risk classification framework), not 「風險管理框架」 (risk management framework). These are distinct concepts.
  - **Fix**: "數位發展部應參考國際標準或規範，推動與國際介接之人工智慧**風險分類框架**"
  - **Source**: [行政院](https://www.ey.gov.tw/Page/9277F759E41CCD91/5d673d1e-f418-47dc-ab35-a06600f77f07), [關鍵評論網](https://www.thenewslens.com/article/262871)
  - **Exam risk**: HIGH -- the exact term from the law is a likely exam question

- [ERROR] "《生成式人工智慧服務管理暫行辦法》**(2023年7月10日)**"
  - **What's wrong**: 7月10日 is the signing date (簽署日); the official publication date (公布日) is **2023年7月13日**. When referring to a regulation's date in an exam context, the publication date is the standard reference.
  - **Fix**: "《生成式人工智慧服務管理暫行辦法》(2023年7月13日公布)" or note both: "2023年7月10日簽署，7月13日公布"
  - **Source**: [國家網信辦](https://www.cac.gov.cn/2023-07/13/c_1690898327029107.htm)
  - **Exam risk**: LOW -- the exam is unlikely to test the exact date of a Chinese regulation, but accuracy matters
