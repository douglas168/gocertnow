## Prompt

Create a clean, modern organizational chart / ecosystem map showing Taiwan's AI governance structure under the 《人工智慧基本法》.

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy), with glowing accent colors and connecting lines. Modern, flat design. Nodes should look like rounded cards with soft shadows. The overall aesthetic should feel like a tech startup landing page — sleek and professional.

**Layout:** Top-down hierarchy with the following structure:

**Level 1 (top center, gold glow #eab308):**
- Large card: "行政院長" with subtitle "國家 AI 戰略特別委員會 召集人"
- Crown or star icon to indicate highest authority

**Level 2 (two cards side by side, connected by lines from Level 1):**

- Left card (blue glow #3b82f6, highlighted as key exam point with 🔥):
  "國科會（NSTC）"
  Subtitle: "中央主管機關"
  Bullet points inside card:
  - "統籌 AI 政策方向"
  - "委員會幕僚作業"

- Right card (purple glow #a78bfa, highlighted with 🔥):
  "數位發展部（MODA）"
  Subtitle: "風險分類框架 + AI 產品評測"
  Bullet points inside card:
  - "建立風險分類框架"
  - "AI 產品與系統評測"

**Level 3 (three cards in a row, connected from Level 2 with dotted lines):**
- Card 1 (teal glow #14b8a6): "金管會" — "金融業 AI 指引"
- Card 2 (teal glow): "衛福部" — "醫療 AI 規範"
- Card 3 (teal glow): "教育部" — "教育 AI 規範"
- A "..." indicator suggesting more ministries

**Level 4 (bottom, subtle gray):**
- A wide bar: "產業界 + 學界 + 公民社會" with subtitle "利害關係人參與"

**Additional elements:**
- The connection lines between Level 1→2 should be solid (direct authority)
- The connection lines between Level 2→3 should be dotted (coordination/guidance)
- A small legend in the bottom-left explaining: solid line = 直接管轄, dotted line = 協調指導

**Text:** All in Traditional Chinese with English abbreviations in parentheses. Clean sans-serif font.

**Dimensions:** 1920x1080 (16:9 landscape), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
