## Prompt

Create a clean, modern Venn diagram showing the relationship between three key AI concepts: AI Governance (AI 治理), AI Regulation (AI 監管), and AI Ethics (AI 倫理).

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy), with translucent colored circles that overlap. Modern, flat design with soft glow effects. The overall aesthetic should feel like a tech startup landing page — sleek and professional.

**Layout:** Three overlapping circles arranged in a classic Venn diagram pattern, but with AI Governance as the LARGEST circle that encompasses the other two (to show that governance is the broadest concept).

**Three circles:**

1. **Largest circle (outer, blue glow #3b82f6, semi-transparent):**
   - Label: "AI 治理（AI Governance）"
   - Subtitle: "最廣的概念"
   - Inside (non-overlapping area): "組織制度、自律規範、國際標準、技術標準、產業最佳實踐"
   - Metaphor annotation: "整個學校的管理體系"

2. **Medium circle (inside the large circle, left side, red/orange glow #f97316, semi-transparent):**
   - Label: "AI 監管（AI Regulation）"
   - Subtitle: "法律層面"
   - Inside (non-overlapping area): "法律、法規、行政命令"
   - Examples: "EU AI Act、台灣 AI 基本法"
   - Metaphor annotation: "校規（有罰則）"
   - Key attribute badge: "有法律強制力"

3. **Medium circle (inside the large circle, right side, purple glow #a78bfa, semi-transparent):**
   - Label: "AI 倫理（AI Ethics）"
   - Subtitle: "價值觀層面"
   - Inside (non-overlapping area): "道德原則、公平觀念、社會責任"
   - Examples: "OECD 原則、UNESCO 建議"
   - Metaphor annotation: "做人的道理"
   - Key attribute badge: "通常無強制力"

**Overlap area (where Regulation and Ethics meet, inside Governance):**
- Label: "法律中的倫理要求"
- Example: "EU AI Act 的公平性要求"

**Additional elements:**
- A table below the Venn diagram comparing the three concepts:
  | | 制定者 | 強制力 | 台灣舉例 |
  |---|---|---|---|
  | 治理 | 多方利害關係人 | 部分有 | 整體 AI 政策 |
  | 監管 | 政府 | 有 | AI 基本法 |
  | 倫理 | 學界 + 社會 | 通常沒有 | OECD/UNESCO |
