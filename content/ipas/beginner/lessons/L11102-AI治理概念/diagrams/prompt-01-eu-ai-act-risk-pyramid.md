## Prompt

Create a clean, modern pyramid/triangle diagram showing the EU AI Act's four risk tiers for AI systems.

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy), with colored horizontal bands forming a pyramid. Modern, flat design with subtle gradients and soft glow effects. The overall aesthetic should feel like a tech startup landing page — sleek and professional.

**Layout:** A pyramid divided into 4 horizontal tiers, widest at the bottom, narrowest at the top. Each tier has a distinct color and clear labels. The pyramid should be vertically oriented with the most dangerous tier at the top.

**Four tiers (top to bottom):**

1. **Top tier (narrowest, red glow #ef4444):** "不可接受風險（Unacceptable Risk）"
   - Label on the right: "完全禁止"
   - Small icons/examples: crossed-out surveillance camera, crossed-out social credit score
   - Examples text: "社會信用評分、大規模監控、潛意識操縱"

2. **Second tier (orange glow #f97316):** "高風險（High Risk）"
   - Label on the right: "嚴格合規 + 人為監督"
   - Small icons: medical cross, briefcase, scales of justice
   - Examples text: "醫療診斷、招聘篩選、信用評分、執法"

3. **Third tier (yellow glow #eab308):** "有限風險（Limited Risk）"
   - Label on the right: "透明度義務（須標示）"
   - Small icons: chat bubble, video camera
   - Examples text: "聊天機器人、Deepfake、生成式 AI"

4. **Bottom tier (widest, green glow #22c55e):** "最小風險（Minimal Risk）"
   - Label on the right: "無強制要求"
   - Small icons: email envelope, game controller
   - Examples text: "垃圾郵件過濾、遊戲 NPC"

**Additional elements:**
- A vertical arrow on the left side labeled "風險等級" (top = high, bottom = low) and "管制強度" (top = strict, bottom = none)
- A small timeline bar at the bottom showing key dates: "2024/8 生效 → 2025/2 禁止類適用 → 2026/8 高風險適用"
- Bottom-right corner: small text "LevelCert.com | IPAS AI應用規劃師 初級"

**Text:** All labels in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) with English terms in parentheses. Font should be clean sans-serif.

**Dimensions:** 1200x1600 (portrait, 3:4 ratio), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
