# Gemini Image Prompt: Three Domains Overview

## Prompt

Create an educational poster showing the three domains of Generative AI as three tall pillars/columns standing side by side. Dark background (#0f172a navy-black). 16:9 aspect ratio.

Layout: Three equal-width vertical pillars with rounded tops, evenly spaced across the image.

Left pillar — "文本生成" (Text Generation):
- Color: Blue (#3b82f6) gradient pillar
- Large icon at top: a document/pen icon
- English label below icon: "Text Generation"
- Example outputs listed vertically inside the pillar: "文章撰寫", "對話問答", "翻譯摘要", "程式碼"
- Representative tools at the base: "ChatGPT / Gemini / OpenAI API"

Center pillar — "圖像生成" (Image Generation):
- Color: Purple (#a855f7) gradient pillar
- Large icon at top: an artist palette icon
- English label below icon: "Image Generation"
- Example outputs listed vertically inside the pillar: "AI繪圖", "風格轉換", "海報設計", "圖片編輯"
- Representative tools at the base: "Midjourney / GPT Image"

Right pillar — "聲音生成" (Audio Generation):
- Color: Orange (#f97316) gradient pillar
- Large icon at top: a speaker/waveform icon
- English label below icon: "Audio Generation"
- Example outputs listed vertically inside the pillar: "文字轉語音", "AI配音", "音樂創作", "歌曲生成"
- Representative tools at the base: "ElevenLabs / Suno AI"

Above the three pillars, a horizontal bar or arrow labeled "輸入：Prompt / 指令" (Input: Prompt) flowing down into all three pillars, showing that all three domains start from a text prompt.

Below the three pillars, a subtle footer showing "多模態工具 (Multimodal)" with Gemini and ChatGPT Plus logos/names, with dotted lines connecting to multiple pillars to show they span domains.

Title at top center in Traditional Chinese: "生成式AI三大應用領域" with English subtitle "Three Domains of Generative AI"

Style: clean, modern, educational infographic. Flat design with subtle depth/shadows on pillars. Sans-serif font. Professional certification study material aesthetic. Dark theme consistent with tech education.

All Chinese text must be in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文), not Simplified.

## Negative Prompts

- No 3D realistic renders
- No photographs or real people
- No cluttered backgrounds or decorative borders
- No Simplified Chinese characters
- No cartoon/childish illustration style
- No excessive text or paragraphs — keep labels concise
- No watermarks
