# Prompt: ANI / AGI / ASI Capability Pyramid

## Instructions for Gemini
Paste the prompt below into Gemini Pro image generation.

---

## Prompt

Create a clean, modern pyramid/triangle diagram showing the three levels of AI capability classification.

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy). The pyramid should have a glowing, futuristic aesthetic with semi-transparent layers. Modern flat design with subtle gradients.

**Layout:** A three-tier pyramid, widest at the bottom, narrowing to the top. Each tier is a distinct layer.

**Three tiers (bottom to top):**

1. **Bottom tier (largest, brightest):** 
   - Label: "窄AI / 弱AI（Artificial Narrow Intelligence, ANI）"
   - Color: Bright blue (#3b82f6) with full opacity — this is the "real" layer
   - Sub-label: "✅ 目前所有 AI 都在這裡"
   - Examples shown as small icons/tags: "ChatGPT", "Siri", "自駕車", "推薦演算法"
   - A glowing highlight/badge: "🔥 考試重點：所有現存 AI 都是 ANI"

2. **Middle tier:**
   - Label: "通用AI（Artificial General Intelligence, AGI）"
   - Color: Purple (#8b5cf6) with 60% opacity — semi-transparent to indicate "not yet real"
   - Sub-label: "❌ 尚未實現 — 理論階段"
   - Description: "能像人類一樣跨領域思考和學習"
   - A dashed/dotted border to indicate hypothetical

3. **Top tier (smallest):**
   - Label: "超級AI（Artificial Super Intelligence, ASI）"
   - Color: Red/gold (#ef4444 or #eab308) with 30% opacity — very faded, almost ghostly
   - Sub-label: "❌ 純粹理論 — 科幻等級"
   - Description: "超越全人類智慧總和"
   - Very faint/ghostly rendering to emphasize this is purely theoretical

**Additional elements:**
- A vertical arrow on the right side labeled "智慧程度（Intelligence Level）↑"
- A horizontal dividing line between ANI and AGI labeled "—— 目前的天花板 ——" (current ceiling)
- A small callout box: "⚠️ 考試陷阱：ChatGPT 看起來很聰明，但仍然是 ANI，不是 AGI！"
- Bottom-right corner: small text "LevelCert.com | IPAS AI應用規劃師 初級"

**Text:** All in Traditional Chinese with English in parentheses. Clean sans-serif font.

**Dimensions:** 1080x1350 (portrait, 4:5 ratio), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson or sharing on social media.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
