## Prompt

Create a clean, modern concentric circles (nested circles / Venn-style) diagram showing the hierarchical relationship between AI, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy). Glowing, futuristic aesthetic. Each circle should have a soft glow and semi-transparent fill. Modern flat design.

**Layout:** Three concentric circles, centered, with the largest on the outside and smallest in the center.

**Three circles (outside to inside):**

1. **Outer circle (largest):**
   - Label at top: "人工智慧（Artificial Intelligence, AI）"
   - Color: Blue (#3b82f6) with 20% fill opacity, glowing border
   - Description placed in the outer ring area (between outer and middle circle): "讓機器展現智慧的所有方法"
   - Examples in the outer ring: "專家系統（Expert Systems）", "規則式 AI（Rule-based AI）"

2. **Middle circle:**
   - Label at top: "機器學習（Machine Learning, ML）"
   - Color: Purple (#8b5cf6) with 30% fill opacity, glowing border
   - Description in the middle ring: "讓機器從資料中自動學習"
   - Examples in the middle ring: "從資料學習的各種演算法"

3. **Inner circle (smallest):**
   - Label at center: "深度學習（Deep Learning, DL）"
   - Color: Gold/yellow (#eab308) with 40% fill opacity, glowing border
   - Description below label: "用多層神經網路學習複雜特徵"
   - The brightest/most emphasized circle to show it's the current hot area

**Additional elements:**
- Arrows pointing inward from each circle with labels:
  - From outer to middle: "子集（Subset）→"
  - From middle to inner: "子集（Subset）→"
- A callout box at bottom: "🔥 考試重點：AI ⊃ ML ⊃ DL — 每個都是前一個的子集，不是同義詞！"
- A small annotation near the outer circle: "包含規則式 AI — 不需要從資料學習"
- A small annotation near the inner circle: "2012 年後爆發式成長"
- Bottom-right corner: small text "LevelCert.com | IPAS AI應用規劃師 初級"

**Text:** All in Traditional Chinese with English in parentheses. Clean sans-serif font.

**Dimensions:** 1200x1200 (square), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
