## Prompt

Create a clean, modern infographic timeline of AI history milestones. The design should be:

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy), with glowing accent colors. Modern, flat design with subtle gradients. The overall aesthetic should feel like a tech startup landing page — sleek and professional.

**Layout:** Horizontal timeline flowing left to right, with events placed alternately above and below the timeline line. The timeline line itself should glow softly.

**Color coding:**
- Blue glow (#3b82f6) for breakthroughs/positive milestones
- Red/orange glow (#ef4444) for AI Winters (downturns)
- Gold/yellow glow (#eab308) for the most important milestones (mark with a star icon)

**Milestones to include (left to right):**

1. **1950** — Turing Test proposed (Alan Turing) [blue]
2. **1956** — Dartmouth Conference: "AI" coined [gold star — most important]
3. **1970s** — 1st AI Winter [red, with downward arrow]
4. **1980s** — Expert Systems era [blue]
5. **Late 1980s** — 2nd AI Winter [red, with downward arrow]
6. **1997** — Deep Blue beats Kasparov [blue]
7. **2012** — AlexNet: Deep Learning revolution [gold star]
8. **2016** — AlphaGo beats Lee Sedol [blue]
9. **2017** — Transformer paper published [blue]
10. **2022** — ChatGPT: GenAI era begins [gold star]
11. **2026** — Taiwan AI Basic Law enacted [blue]

**Text:** All labels in Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) with English terms in parentheses. Font should be clean sans-serif. Example: "達特茅斯會議（Dartmouth Conference）"

**Additional elements:**
- A subtle upward trend line showing "AI capability" growing exponentially after 2012
- Small icons next to key events (chess piece for Deep Blue, brain icon for Dartmouth, chat bubble for ChatGPT)
- Bottom-right corner: small text "LevelCert.com | IPAS AI應用規劃師 初級"

**Dimensions:** 1920x1080 (16:9 landscape), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
