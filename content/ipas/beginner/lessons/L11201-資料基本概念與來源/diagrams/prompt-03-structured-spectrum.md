## Prompt

Create a clean, modern horizontal spectrum/gradient diagram showing the three levels of data structure, with real-world file format examples positioned along the spectrum.

**Style:** Dark theme (background #0a0a0f or similar dark navy), with a horizontal gradient bar as the centerpiece. Modern, flat design. The overall aesthetic should feel like a tech startup landing page — sleek and professional.

**Layout:** A wide horizontal gradient bar in the center, transitioning from green (left, structured) through yellow (center, semi-structured) to red (right, unstructured). File format examples are positioned above and below the bar at their correct positions.

**Three zones on the spectrum:**

1. **Left zone (green glow #22c55e):** "結構化（Structured）"
   - Subtitle: "固定表格 schema，行與列"
   - Example formats positioned above the bar:
     - "Excel 試算表" (icon: spreadsheet)
     - "CSV 檔案" (icon: comma-separated text)
     - "SQL 資料庫" (icon: database cylinder)
   - Label below: "~20% of all data"

2. **Center zone (yellow glow #eab308):** "半結構化（Semi-structured）"
   - Subtitle: "有標籤/鍵值對，無固定 schema"
   - Example formats:
     - "JSON" (icon: curly braces) — with a 🔥 marker and callout: "考試陷阱：JSON 不是非結構化！"
     - "XML" (icon: angle brackets)
     - "HTML" (icon: web page)
     - "電子郵件 Email" (icon: envelope) — with callout: "有結構化欄位 + 自由文字內文"
   - Label below: "The tricky middle ground"

3. **Right zone (red glow #ef4444):** "非結構化（Unstructured）"
   - Subtitle: "無預定義格式"
   - Example formats:
     - "圖片 JPEG/PNG" (icon: image)
     - "影片 MP4" (icon: video)
     - "音訊 MP3" (icon: audio wave)
     - "PDF 文件" (icon: document)
     - "社群貼文" (icon: chat bubble)
   - Label below: "~80% of all data"

**Additional elements:**
- An arrow spanning the full width at the very top, labeled "結構化程度（Degree of Structure）" from "高（High）" on the left to "低（Low）" on the right
- A pie chart in the bottom-left corner showing the 20/80 split between structured and unstructured data
- Bottom-right corner: small text "LevelCert.com | IPAS AI應用規劃師 初級"

**Text:** All in Traditional Chinese with English in parentheses. Clean sans-serif font.

**Dimensions:** 1920x1080 (16:9 landscape), suitable for embedding in a web-based course lesson.

**Do NOT include:** Any watermarks, stock photo elements, or photorealistic humans.
