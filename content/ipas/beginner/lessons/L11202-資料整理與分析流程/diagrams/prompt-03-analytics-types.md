# Diagram Prompt: 4 Analytics Types Progression

**For:** Gemini 2.0 Flash image generation
**Use in:** study-guide.md Section 3-4

## Prompt

Create a clean infographic showing the 4 types of data analytics as a staircase/pyramid progressing from left to right, bottom to top. Style: flat design, dark background (#0F172A).

4 ascending steps, each a different color:

Step 1 (bottom-left, blue): "描述性分析 Descriptive Analytics"
- Question: "發生了什麼？ What happened?"
- Analogy icon: rear-view mirror (看後照鏡)
- Difficulty: 1 star

Step 2 (green): "診斷性分析 Diagnostic Analytics"
- Question: "為什麼發生？ Why did it happen?"
- Analogy icon: magnifying glass (找原因)
- Difficulty: 2 stars

Step 3 (orange): "預測性分析 Predictive Analytics"
- Question: "未來會怎樣？ What will happen?"
- Analogy icon: crystal ball (看水晶球)
- Difficulty: 3 stars

Step 4 (top-right, purple): "指示性分析 Prescriptive Analytics"
- Question: "該怎麼做？ What should we do?"
- Analogy icon: GPS navigation (給導航)
- Difficulty: 4 stars

Show an upward arrow along the side labeled "分析深度 / 難度 →". Text in Traditional Chinese + English. Dimensions: 1200x700px. No watermarks.
