# Gemini Image Prompt: AI 導入成本冰山圖

## Prompt

Create a professional infographic illustration of an **iceberg metaphor** representing the costs of adopting generative AI in an enterprise.

### Composition
- A large iceberg in the center of the image, partially submerged in water
- The waterline clearly divides the iceberg into a small visible portion (above water) and a much larger hidden portion (below water)
- The above-water portion should be approximately 10-15% of the total iceberg volume
- The below-water portion should be approximately 85-90% of the total iceberg volume

### Above the waterline — Visible Costs (顯性成本)
Label this section "顯性成本 Visible Costs（僅佔 5-15%）"

Place these items as clean text labels with icons next to the visible portion:
- API 費用 / 訂閱費 (API & subscription fees)
- 雲端運算費用 (cloud computing costs)
- 軟體授權費 (software licenses)

### Below the waterline — Hidden Costs (隱藏成本)
Label this section "隱藏成本 Hidden Costs（佔 85-95%）🔥"

Place these items as clean text labels with icons next to the submerged portion, distributed across the large underwater area:
- 資料收集、清理、標註 (data collection, cleaning, labeling)
- 員工教育訓練 (employee training)
- 系統整合與客製開發 (system integration & custom development)
- RAG / 知識庫建置 (RAG / knowledge base setup)
- 合規與資安審查 (compliance & security audit)
- 組織變革管理 (organizational change management)
- 持續維護與模型更新 (ongoing maintenance & model updates)

### Key callout
Add a prominent callout box or badge somewhere on the image:
"API 費用只佔總成本的 5-15%，真正的大頭是隱藏成本！"

### Style
- **Background:** Dark navy/charcoal gradient (#0f1724 to #1a2332), professional and modern
- **Iceberg above water:** Light blue/white, clean and bright
- **Iceberg below water:** Deep blue/teal, slightly transparent to show the massive underwater volume
- **Waterline:** Clear, slightly glowing cyan line
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif font, Traditional Chinese with English subtitles
- **Overall feel:** Professional corporate infographic suitable for an educational course, not cartoonish
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 landscape
- **No people or characters** in the image

### Language
All text in **Traditional Chinese** with English terms in parentheses where shown above.
