# Gemini Image Prompt: AI 導入四階段旅程圖

## Prompt

Create a professional **timeline journey map infographic** showing the 4 phases of AI adoption based on the MOEA (經濟部產業發展署)《AI導入指引》framework.

### Layout
- Horizontal timeline flowing left to right
- Four milestone stations along the path, connected by a winding road or path
- Each milestone is a distinct visual station with an icon, phase name, and key activities
- Progressive visual feel: the path gets brighter/more developed as it moves right, symbolizing maturity

### Phase 1 — 構想評估 (Ideation & Assessment)
- Icon: lightbulb or magnifying glass
- Subtitle: "我們需要 AI 嗎？"
- Key items (small text below):
  - 痛點分析
  - 資源盤點
  - 場景選定
- Output badge: "應用場景清單"

### Phase 2 — 方案設計 (Solution Design)
- Icon: blueprint or pencil/ruler
- Subtitle: "怎麼做？用什麼？"
- Key items:
  - KPI 定義
  - 資料需求分析
  - 成本估算
- Output badge: "專案計畫書"

### Phase 3 — 驗證 POC (Proof of Concept)
- Icon: flask/beaker or test tube
- Subtitle: "真的可行嗎？"
- Key items:
  - 原型開發
  - 模擬營運測試
  - ROI 驗證
- Output badge: "POC 報告"

### Phase 4 — 實施營運 (Deployment & Operations)
- Icon: rocket launch or factory/gear
- Subtitle: "如何規模化？"
- Key items:
  - 正式部署上線
  - 持續監控
  - 擴展 AI 文化
- Output badge: "營運中的 AI 系統"

### Additional elements
- A subtle **curved arrow** from Phase 4 back to Phase 1 with the label "可回到前一階段調整" (can loop back to earlier phases)
- The mnemonic "構方驗實" displayed prominently as a subtitle or banner at the top
- A small "不可跳過任何階段！" (Cannot skip any phase!) warning note near the path

### Style
- **Background:** Dark navy/charcoal gradient (#0f1724 to #1a2332), professional and modern
- **Path/road color:** Gradient from cool blue (Phase 1) to warm green (Phase 4), showing progression
- **Milestone markers:** Circular or hexagonal badges, each with a subtle glow
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif font, Traditional Chinese as primary text, English in parentheses
- **Overall feel:** Professional educational infographic, suitable for a certification course
- **Aspect ratio:** 16:9 landscape (wider than tall)
- **No people or characters** in the image

### Language
All text in **Traditional Chinese** with English terms in parentheses where shown above.
