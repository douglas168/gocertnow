**X-axis: Format flexibility**
- Left: Fixed classroom (工研院, 商研院, 政大)
- Middle: Hybrid (Tiandiren, iSpan)
- Right: Self-paced 24/7 (HR Only One, TAIA, you)

- Bottom: No guarantee (most competitors)

**Where LevelCert lands:** Top-right corner. Empty quadrant. That's your moat.

### Red flags to watch for

Mark with ⚠️ if you find any of these in a competitor's pitch — they're things you need to neutralize on your landing page:

- [ ] Any competitor offers >30-day video access
- [ ] Any competitor has AI-powered explanations
- [ ] Any competitor has a pass guarantee
- [ ] Any competitor is priced below NT$3,980 (outside of intro offers)
- [ ] Any competitor has alumni testimonials with real names + scores
- [ ] Any competitor has a published pass rate
- [ ] Any competitor targets students specifically

Each ⚠️ you find = a specific counter-message you need on your landing page.

## How to speed up the research

1. **Open all 10 URLs in separate browser tabs** before starting.
2. **Use ⌘+F to search each page** for keywords: 價格, 退費, 保證, 學生, 時數, 線上, 實體, 模擬考, 講義, 延長, 期限.
3. **Screenshot the pricing section of each** into `docs/_todo/competitor-screenshots/` (you'll want these for reference).
4. **Set a timer: 10 minutes per competitor, hard limit.** If you're going longer, you're over-researching. Move on.
5. **Don't sign up for their mailing lists during research** — you'll get polluted comparison emails. Do that separately if you want to study their email funnels.

---

## What this research is NOT for

- ❌ Not for building a "competitor comparison table" marketing page. That usually hurts you more than helps.
- ❌ Not for copying their copy. Your voice has to be yours.
- ❌ Not for deciding whether to launch. You're launching regardless.
- ❌ Not for generating new features ideas. You already have your 4-moat focus — don't get distracted by competitor feature bloat.

The purpose is single: **confirm your moat is real, and find the 1–2 concrete things you need to neutralize on your landing page.**
