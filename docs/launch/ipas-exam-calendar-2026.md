## What this changes in the launch plan

### 1. Founder exam date is 2026-05-**23**, not 05-25
All previous docs that said 5/25 were my error — corrected to 5/23 across ROADMAP, TODO, START-HERE, HANDOFF, landing-page-copy, build-in-public-calendar, memory files, and CLAUDE.md.

### 2. "IPAS runs ~3× per year" rationale was wrong
- **初級 runs 4× per year** (Mar, May, Aug, Nov — roughly every 2–3 months).
- **中級 runs 2× per year** (May, Nov — exactly 6 months apart).
- The 6-month extension policy is still the right call, but the binding constraint is **中級's 6-month gap**, not 初級. A failed 中級 student using a 3-month extension would never reach the next exam. This rationale is the one to cite in marketing and FAQ copy.

### 3. Pre-order access vs first-exam timing — DECIDED 2026-04-10
With a vanilla **3-month initial access** clock, anyone who pre-orders in Apr–May and misses the 4/10 registration cutoff would have their access expire **before** they could sit their first real exam (Aug 15). Example:

| Purchase date | 3-mo access expires | First sittable exam | Gap |
|---|---|---|---|
| 2026-04-16 (Week 1 buyer) | 2026-07-16 | 2026-08-15 | **-30 days** |
| 2026-05-01 | 2026-08-01 | 2026-08-15 | **-14 days** |
| 2026-05-15 | 2026-08-15 | 2026-08-15 | 0 days buffer |
| 2026-06-01 | 2026-09-01 | 2026-08-15 | +15 days ✓ |

**Decision (2026-04-10):** All pre-launch tiers — 社團, 學生, 創始會員, 早鳥 — get access locked **through 2026-12-31** regardless of purchase date. This guarantees every pre-launch buyer can attempt **both** the Aug 15 AND Nov 7 exams from a single purchase, with zero extension paperwork. 正價 (seat 71+) reverts to the standard 3-month initial + 6-month-on-fail extension policy. Market line: 「創始 / 早鳥會員享完整課程存取至 2026 年底，保證至少兩次正式考試機會。」

### 4. University club outreach moves from June → September
The ROADMAP originally said club outreach starts ~2026-06-01. That was wrong — you don't have alumni passes until **2026-09-02** (the first 初級 第三次 results). Earliest credible outreach date is **2026-09-05**, targeting the fall-semester club planning window. June / July is the wrong moment — clubs are on summer break and you have no testimonials.

### 5. Phase 2 retiming
Old: "Phase 2: May 26 – Jun 22 — First Alumni + Club Outreach" assumed a June exam. New reality:
- **May 26 – Sep 1:** Build-in-public recovery + 初級 第三次 student prep sprint (cohort sits Aug 15).
- **Sep 2 – Sep 5:** Collect first real alumni testimonials.
- **Sep 5 – Oct 31:** University club outreach window (aligned with fall semester).
- **Nov 7 / Nov 14:** Q4 exam cohorts sit (初級 第四次 + 中級 第二次).
- **Nov 25:** Second alumni batch lands; year-end scale moment.
