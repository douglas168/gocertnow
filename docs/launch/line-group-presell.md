
### If someone asks a question
- Answer in the group (so everyone sees)
- Be specific, no marketing speak
- End with: *"還有問題都歡迎繼續問 🙌"*

### If someone is hesitant
- Do NOT push
- Offer free diagnostic: *"要不要先做免費診斷考試？20 題，會告訴你現在的程度，完全不用付錢：[URL]"*
- Follow up 1 week later ONCE, then leave them alone

### If the group owner/admin pushes back on "selling"
- Apologize, offer to delete the post
- Pivot: *"完全理解。那如果有人有 iPAS 準備問題歡迎私訊我個人，我都免費回答，畢竟剛考完 🙏"*
- Never argue in the group

## Pre-post checklist

- [ ] Replace all `[placeholders]` with real info
- [ ] Check the landing page URL works on mobile
- [ ] Make sure your Stripe / LINE Pay / transfer info is ready BEFORE posting (people will ask within minutes)
- [ ] Have the founding cohort welcome DM template ready
- [ ] Tell the group admin privately BEFORE posting, out of courtesy: *"我準備在群裡分享我做的課程，希望不會打擾到大家。如果不合適請直接告訴我。"*
- [ ] Post during peak hours: 12:00-13:00 lunch break or 20:00-22:00 evening
- [ ] Don't post on Monday morning or Friday night (lowest engagement)
- [ ] Pin the landing page link in your own LINE profile before posting

## Success metrics

- **Within 2 hours:** At least 3 reactions (emoji, reply, or private message)
- **Within 24 hours:** At least 1 "+1" or direct payment question
- **Within 7 days:** At least 3 paying founding seats
- **Within 14 days:** At least 5 paying founding seats (Week 2 goal)

If you hit <1 response within 24h, the message is wrong. Rewrite from scratch using real quotes from the customer discovery you did in Week 1. Do NOT just re-post the same message.

---

## One hard rule

**Do not post in more than one LINE group with the same message.** Group owners talk, and getting flagged as spam in the Taiwan IPAS community would damage your reputation for months. If you have access to more than one group, write a custom message for each that references the specific group context.
