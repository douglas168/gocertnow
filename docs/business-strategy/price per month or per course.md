# should I price per month or per course?  tell me the pros and cons? and your recommendation?  you can askuserquestions.

**Recommendation**: Price **per course** (one-time payment, NT\$4,999–7,999 per certification path) with an **upsell to unlimited access** (NT\$999/month) as the growth lever.

Here's why, with pros/cons for each model:

## Per Month (Subscription)

| Pros | Cons |
| :-- | :-- |
| Predictable MRR revenue | High churn risk (cert prep is finite) |
| Lower entry barrier | "Subscription fatigue" in edtech |
| Upsell opportunities | Users cancel after passing exam |
| Data for retention experiments | LTV uncertainty |

## Per Course (One-Time)

| Pros | Cons |
| :-- | :-- |
| Matches buyer psychology ("I need this one cert") | Lumpy revenue |
| Higher LTV per sale (NT\$5K vs NT\$1K) | Harder MRR storytelling |
| Lower churn anxiety | Needs strong upsell to repeat buyers |
| Easier guarantee ("pass this course") | Slower to \$3K MRR |

## Why Per Course Wins for LevelCert

1. **Cert prep is transactional**: Users buy to solve "pass iPAS" not "subscribe forever."
2. **Higher conversion**: NT\$5K one-time beats NT\$599/month psychologically.
3. **Upsell path**: "Congrats on iPAS! Next cert for NT\$3,999 (50% off)."
4. **Proven model**: Udemy, local courses use one-time pricing [–7].
5. **Guarantee friendly**: "Pass iPAS or full refund" is clearer than subscription churn.

## Recommended Structure

```
Core Offer: NT$5,999 per certification path (iPAS AI, AWS AI Practitioner, etc.)
- Unlimited access for 6 months
- Personalized quests, mocks, guild
- Pass guarantee

Upsell 1: NT$999/month unlimited (all paths)
Upsell 2: NT$1,499 one-time "Elite" (private mentor + lifetime)

Free: Diagnostic + first quest
```

**Expected results**:

- 10% conversion from diagnostic → \$5K sale = \$50K revenue from 100 leads.
- 20% upsell to unlimited = recurring tail.

**Test both**: Run A/B test (course vs subscription) on first 100 visitors.
