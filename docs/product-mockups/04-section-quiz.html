AI Explanation

While Turing's 1950 paper laid important groundwork, the Dartmouth Conference in 1956 is officially recognized as the birth of AI as a formal field. This is where John McCarthy coined the term "Artificial Intelligence" and researchers agreed to pursue it as a distinct discipline.
