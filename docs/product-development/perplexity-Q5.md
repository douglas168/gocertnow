## 5. **Failed Case: Generic AI Course Factory (Anonymous Solo Founder)** – AI Content → Death by Commoditization

**Workflow Asset:** AI-generated courses (text + video) on hot topics (AI, crypto, dropshipping)

| Dimension                          | Details                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **What they built**                | 20+ AI-generated courses on trending topics, sold via Udemy/Teachable. Minimal human input, generic voice [xperiencify](https://xperiencify.com/ai-created-courses-are-they-taking-over-or-destined-to-fail-heres-what-you-need-to-know-for-2024-beyond/)                                                                                                           |
| **AI workflow as moat**            | **None**: Used off-the-shelf AI tools (ChatGPT + Descript + Canva) with generic prompts. No proprietary data, no audience trust, no distribution [xperiencify](https://xperiencify.com/ai-created-courses-are-they-taking-over-or-destined-to-fail-heres-what-you-need-to-know-for-2024-beyond/)                                                                    |
| **Monetization endgame attempted** | **Scale own output**: Flood market with low-cost courses ($20–$50). Expected volume to compensate for low quality [xperiencify](https://xperiencify.com/ai-created-courses-are-they-taking-over-or-destined-to-fail-heres-what-you-need-to-know-for-2024-beyond/)                                                                                                   |
| **Why it failed**                  | **Content-to-revenue gap**: High-volume AI content built no authority. Refund rates through the roof (~40%), only 5% course completion. Couldn’t compete with authenticated experts. Tools commoditized in 6 months [xperiencify](https://xperiencify.com/ai-created-courses-are-they-taking-over-or-destined-to-fail-heres-what-you-need-to-know-for-2024-beyond/) |
| **Fatal flaw**                     | **No workflow moat**: Anyone could replicate the exact same workflow. No proprietary data, no unique voice, no audience. 95% of AI pilots fail to deliver business value; this was a cost center, not revenue engine [ainvest](https://www.ainvest.com/news/assessing-scalability-ai-powered-solo-founder-businesses-2602/)                                         |
