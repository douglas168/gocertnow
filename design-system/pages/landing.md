# Page Override: Landing (`/`)

> Rules in this file **override** `design-system/MASTER.md`. For anything not specified here, follow MASTER.md.

---

**Route:** `/`
**Purpose:** Convert visitors into waitlist signups (pre-launch) or course purchasers (post-launch)
**RPG elements:** Selling point only — show screenshots of gamified dashboard

---

## Layout Override

- **Max width:** `max-w-7xl` (wider than default `max-w-6xl`)

## Section Order

| # | Section | Content | CTA |
|---|---------|---------|-----|
| 1 | **Hero** | Headline + subtitle + primary CTA + gamified dashboard screenshot | Waitlist / Purchase |
| 2 | **Problem** | "Cert prep is boring, overwhelming, and lonely" | — |
| 3 | **Solution** | 3-4 feature cards: micro-lessons, mob battles, boss battle exam, weakness radar | — |
| 4 | **How It Works** | 3-step visual: Learn -> Battle -> Certify | — |
| 5 | **Social Proof** | 3-5 testimonials (photo + name + cert achieved) + pass rate stat | — |
| 6 | **Course Preview** | Module outline with lesson count | — |
| 7 | **Pricing** | Single tier, clear price, 3-month access, pass guarantee | Emerald CTA |
| 8 | **FAQ** | 5-7 questions, accordion | — |
| 9 | **Footer CTA** | "Start your quest" | Repeat primary CTA |

## CTA Placement

- Hero (above fold), post-social-proof, pricing section
- Mobile: sticky bottom bar on scroll past hero

## Visual Overrides

- **Hero background:** Gradient `slate-900` to `slate-950` for depth
- **Feature cards:** RPG accent color icons (amber XP, emerald success, violet badges, indigo radar)
- **Stat counters:** Number count-up animation on scroll-into-view
- **Screenshot:** Gamified dashboard with XP, radar, badges — the key differentiator visual

## Anti-Patterns

- No wall of text — every section scannable in 3 seconds
- No generic stock photos — use product screenshots
- No hidden pricing — show it clearly for trust
